Booking Policies

Guidelines and rules for room bookings

General Booking Rules

  • • Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance
  • • Maximum booking duration is 8 hours per day
  • • Rooms must be vacated on time to avoid conflicts
  • • Equipment must be returned in good condition
  • • No food or drinks allowed in meeting rooms

Cancellation Policy

  • • Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the booking time
  • • Late cancellations may result in booking restrictions
  • • No-shows will be recorded and may affect future booking privileges

Equipment Usage

  • • Podcast equipment requires prior approval
  • • Technical support is available during business hours
  • • Users are responsible for any damage to equipment
  • • Equipment setup should be completed before the booking time
